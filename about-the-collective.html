About the Collective

Established in January 2006, Switch N' Play (SNP) is a Brooklyn drag collective. Queer in every sense of the word, SNP spans the gender spectrum in life and on stage. We have performed at Brooklyn Pride, Manhattan Pride, Hot Rabbit, Burlesque by the Beach at Coney Island, Henrietta Hudson, Murray Hill's Miss Lez Pageant, IDKE, HyperGender, and IVY-Q 2013 & 2014. In 2008, SNP was awarded "Best Performance" at Pride Fest and and they were voted "Best Drag Kings" by GO magazine readers in both 2009 and 2010. In 2015, they were nominated for the Brooklyn Nightlife Awards in the categories of "Best Performance Show" and "Hall of Fame."

Switch N' Play currently produces monthly drag and burlesque shows at Branded Saloon in Prospect Heights. You can find us there every second and fourth Saturday, bringing our guts and glitter to stage alongside our rotating special guests, some of the best burlesque and drag performers in NYC. Our kings also produce a monthly showcase at Bizarre Bushwick called BEEF: a night of MANful performance on last Tuesdays of the month. Check our schedule to catch the next show!

Since its inception ten years ago, SNP has been committed to fostering drag and queer community in Brooklyn by creating safe space for drag, burlesque and other gender-based performance by people of all levels of experience. SNP's Open Drag Nights have united amateur and experienced performers on the same stage since 2006. In 2016, Open Drag returns as "Moxie: a night of invigorating drag & burlesque" at Branded Saloon! Contact us to get involved!

SNP is: Divina GranSparkle, Elle McQueen, K.James, Lee VaLone, Miss Malice, Nyx Nocturne, Pearl Harbor, Ri Zen, Stanley Steamer, and Vigor Mortis.
