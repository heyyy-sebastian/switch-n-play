About the Collective

Switch n' Play (SNP) is a Brooklyn drag collective established in 2006. Queer in every sense of the word, SNP spans the gender spectrum in life and on stage. We currently produce drag and burlesque variety shows twice a month at Branded Saloon in Prospect Heights. In 2015, we were nominated for the Brooklyn Nightlife Awards in the categories of "Best Performance Show" and "Hall of Fame," and in 2016, we were nominated for "Best Burlesque Show." SNP and its members have been featured in the Huffington Post, Posture Magazine, Vice Broadly, and Velour: the Drag Magazine.

You can find at Branded Saloon every second and fourth Saturday, where we joined by our rotating special guests — some of the best burlesque and drag performers in NYC. Check our schedule to catch the next show!

Since its inception ten years ago, SNP has been committed to fostering drag and queer community in Brooklyn by creating safe space for drag, burlesque and other gender-based performance by people of all levels of experience. SNP's Open Drag Nights have united amateur and experienced performers on the same stage since 2006. In 2016, Open Drag returns as "Moxie: a Night of Invigorating Drag & Burlesque" at Branded Saloon! Contact us if you are a new performer, looking for your chance to join our next Moxie cast. In the past, these shows have raised funds for organizations like the Audre Lorde Project and the Queer Detainee Empowerment Project.
